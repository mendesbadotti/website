MB is a retail design studio specialising in creating brands, spaces, and activations that move retail.

We work across the full cycle: consultancy, strategy, branding, design, visual merchandising, communications, campaigns and public relations, whether through integrated support or specific projects, depending on each client’s needs and business stage.

We transform brands into experiences, and connections into opportunities.

Services

We build and reposition brands through an integrated perspective connecting identity, market positioning, and commercial results.

  • Retail branding consultancy
  • Point-of-sale diagnosis
  • Target audience and purchase behaviour research
  • Store positioning and concept
  • Benchmarking and competitor analysis
  • Omnichannel strategy
  • Naming and brand creation from scratch
  • Brand redesign and repositioning
  • Full visual identity
  • Brand guidelines
  • Tone of voice and verbal language
  • Brand architecture
  • Brand storytelling and narrative

We bring the brand to life at every touchpoint, from the physical space to the customer's sensory journey.

  • Visual identity applied to the space
  • Internal and external signage
  • Wayfinding and façade design
  • Product display planning
  • Window display design and seasonal changes
  • Custom displays and product fixtures
  • Visual merchandising guidelines
  • Team training in visual merchandising
  • Team training in customer experience
  • Customer journey mapping
  • Sensory design
  • Phygital experiences
  • Technology integration
  • Special formats: flagship stores, pop-ups, showrooms, corners, and exhibition stands

We elevate brands through campaigns, immersive experiences, and strategic connections.

  • Seasonal and launch campaigns
  • Store opening campaigns
  • Art direction
  • Social media content production
  • Graphic and digital materials
  • Integrated online and offline campaigns
  • Brand activations and promotional experiences
  • Public relations and connection curation
